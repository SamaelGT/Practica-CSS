Preparation Before Vaccine
The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work:
alcoholic beverages

Its consumption, especially if it is excessive, weakens the immune system and, consequently, reduces the ability to deal with infectious diseases, such as the case of COVID-19. Binge drinking includes binge drinking, defined as five or more drinks on a single occasion (within two to three hours) for men and four or more drinks (within two to three hours) for women.

Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on immune work. Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol production and oxidative stress in the body.

Eat healty food

Food is a very important aspect. Balanced nutrition helps reduce the recovery time of the patient with COVID-19 and reduce the complications that could arise in the convalescence process.

Get enough sleep

Get a good night’s sleep, drink plenty of water and eat regularly to keep your blood sugar steady. Taking care of yourself helps your body function properly.

Exercise or physical activity

Maintaining regular physical activity increases the chances of survival in COVID-19 patients by up to eight times compared to those who lead a sedentary life.
